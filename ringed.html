DLC - The Ringed city

The Ringed City is the final expansion for Dark Souls III and the definitive conclusion to the entire Dark Souls story.

Your journey takes you to the very end of time. The world has completely collapsed, and all kingdoms from different eras have melted together into a chaotic pile of ruins called The Dreg Heap. From there, you descend into the legendary, isolated Ringed City—a beautiful, ancient city created by Lord Gwyn at the dawn of the world to keep the Pygmy Lords and the dangerous "Dark Soul of Mankind" hidden away.

Key features

  • Breathtaking and Chaotic Environments: You start by traveling through shattered, vertical ruins falling into the sky, before entering the swampy, massive, and golden Ringed City, which is guarded by a giant dragon and armies of ancient knights.

  • Unbelievable Boss Challenges: This DLC features some of the most spectacular and punishing bosses FromSoftware has ever designed:

    • The Demon Prince: A chaotic battle against two fiery demons that fuse together into a massive, flying nightmare.

    • Darkeater Midir: A colossal, corrupted dragon with a massive health bar. Defeating him is considered a true badge of honor among Dark Souls players.

  • Slave Knight Gael (The Perfect Ending): The final boss of the trilogy is Slave Knight Gael. You fight him in a massive, empty desert of ash at the literal end of the world. He has spent thousands of years consuming the Dark Soul just so a new world can be painted. It is an emotional, fast-paced, and cinematic masterpiece of a fight that perfectly closes the curtains on the entire franchise.
