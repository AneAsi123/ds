DLC - Crown of the Ivory King

Crown of the Ivory King is the final chapter of the "Lost Crowns" trilogy. This expansion changes the atmosphere completely, taking you away from the dark caves and fiery towers to Eleum Loyce, a beautiful but freezing winter city covered in deep snow and thick ice.

The story tells the tragic tale of the Ivory King, a noble ruler who built his entire kingdom directly over the Old Chaos (a gateway to a dangerous hellfire) to keep it contained. To protect his people, he eventually sacrificed himself by jumping into the flames.

Key features:

  • The Freezing Blizzard: When you first arrive, a massive snowstorm completely blocks your view and freezes many pathways. You must defeat a specific boss to stop the storm, which thaws the ice across the city, opening up brand new areas and treasures to explore.

  • Gathering Your Knights: Before facing the final boss, you must explore the frozen city to find the four loyal Loyce Knights who survived. These knights will join you in the final battle to help seal the portals of the Chaos fire.

  • The Grand Cathedral and the Old Chaos: The final battle is incredibly unique. You jump into a massive, glowing pit of fire to fight waves of corrupted knights before facing the Burnt Ivory King himself, who emerges from the flames in an epic, cinematic showdown.
