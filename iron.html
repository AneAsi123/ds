DLC - Crown of the Old Iron King

Crown of the Old Iron King is the second chapter of the "Lost Crowns" trilogy. This expansion takes you to the Brume Tower, a massive, vertical fortress made of iron, stone, and chains. The tower hangs over a volcanic canyon and is completely covered in black ash and thick smoke.

The story follows the tragedy of the Old Iron King, a ruler who became too greedy with his iron industry, causing his massive kingdom to sink into a lake of fire.

Key Features:

  • Vertical Level Design: Brume Tower is all about exploring up and down. You will descend into the depths of the tower using giant elevators, walking across massive chains hanging over deadly drops, and exploring dark rooms filled with industrial machinery.

  • The Ashen Idols: Throughout the tower, you will find creepy ash statues of a queen named Nadalia. These idols curse your character and heal nearby enemies. To defeat the enemies easily, you must first find special "Smelter Wedges" to destroy the statues.

  • Legendary Boss Fights: This DLC contains two of the most popular and difficult bosses in the entire game:

    • Sir Alonne: A honorable, lightning-fast samurai warrior. If you manage to defeat him without taking a single hit, he performs a special unique animation.
    • Fume Knight: A massive knight wielding two giant swords. He is famous among Dark Souls fans for being one of the most brutal and punishing bosses ever created.
