DLC -Crown of the Sunken King

Crown of the Sunken King is the first part of the "Lost Crowns" trilogy. This expansion sends you deep underground to find the crown of King Sunken, an ancient ruler who built a massive civilization hidden away from the surface world.

Your journey takes you to Shulva, Sanctum City, a breathtaking underground city made of giant stone pyramids and temples. The city fell into ruin after a tragic event involving a massive dragon that slept deep inside the caverns.

Key features:

  • Puzzle-Like Level Design: Shulva is unique because the environment is interactive. By hitting hidden glowing pillars with your weapon or arrows, you can raise or lower giant stone structures. This changes the layout of the map, revealing secret paths, treasures, and shortcuts.

  • Tough Environmental Hazards: The enemies here are heavily armored and many of them inflict a deadly toxic poison. You will also encounter phantom soldiers that cannot be damaged until you find and destroy their physical armor rooms hidden nearby.

  • Sinh, the Slumbering Dragon: The final challenge of this DLC is a magnificent battle against Sinh, a massive, ancient dragon that breathes toxic fire. It is considered one of the most intense and cinematic dragon fights in the entire Dark Souls series.
