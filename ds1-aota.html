DLC - Artorias of the Abyss

Artorias of the Abyss is the only expansion for the first Dark Souls game, and it is widely considered one of the best DLCs ever made.

Story-wise, a mysterious force pulls you into the past—hundreds of years before the events of the main game. You travel to the ancient, fallen kingdom of Oolacile, a land that is slowly being destroyed and swallowed by a terrifying, corrupting darkness known as The Abyss.

Key Features:

  • The Legend of Knight Artorias: You finally get to meet Sir Artorias, one of the four legendary knights of Gwyn. In the main game, he is remembered as a flawless hero who walked the Abyss. However, when you meet him, you discover the tragic truth: he sacrificed his shield to protect his wolf companion, became corrupted by the darkness, and lost his mind.

  • Brutal Boss Battles: This DLC features four unique bosses that offer some of the hardest challenges in the entire game. You will fight the aggressive Sanctuary Guardian, the tragic Knight Artorias, the hidden dragon Black Dragon Kalameet, and Manus, Father of the Abyss—the terrifying final boss of the expansion.
